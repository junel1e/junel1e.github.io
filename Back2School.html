Anteaters: Back to School

Being employed at UCI as the graphic designer has given me opportunities to illustrate a multitude of anteaters for different occasions. This selection was for the back-to-school season and was printed onto stickers for first years to be given among their move-in with each design being centered around the respective housing community.
