Anteaters: UCI Workers

Being employed at UCI as their graphic designer has given me opportunities to illustrate a multitude of anteaters for different occasions. This selection was used for UCI related jobs; the mailman was used in the branding of the mailroom, the anteater holding a clipboard and keys was used in housing's hiring campaign and the bottom two were used in housing operations' material about the services they provide.
