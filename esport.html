Bronco Esports

In high school, I co-founded the Esports club with fellow students, where I assumed the responsibility of conceptualizing and implementing the brand across various platforms, building it from the ground up. Our mascot was the Broncos, and I undertook the task of reimagining the logo to align with our school identity while infusing a new sense of professionalism. Additionally, I personally designed all media content on Twitch, ensuring a cohesive and engaging presence, and kept our audience updated with timely game updates on social media platforms. Unfortunately, their social media accounts are currently inactive. However, the club is still operational, and you can find an archive of the all graphics I created for them on their Instagram.
